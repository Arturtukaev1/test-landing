I'm an artist

Welcome to my portfolio website!
I am a photorealist artist based in Los Angeles. My goal as an artist is to create pieces that evoke emotion and leave a lasting impression on viewers. Whether it's a portrait, landscape, or still life, I strive to capture every detail with precision and accuracy.

About me

I have been honing my craft for 30 and have had the privilege of showcasing my work in "Art in Focus" and "Reflecting Life". My passion for photorealism stems from my inspiration and personal motivation.
